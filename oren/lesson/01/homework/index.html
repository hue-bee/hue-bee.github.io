Part D — ELA: Pronoun Clarity & Agreement

4 Questions

For each question, point to the exact word each pronoun replaces. If you can't point to exactly one word, the sentence has a problem.

Q1. Which is correct? "Neither of the coaches submitted ___ roster on time." L2
Q2. Which sentence fixes the unclear pronoun reference? "Maria told her sister that she had won the scholarship." L3
Q3. "Each of the students must bring ___ own permission slip." Which pronoun agrees correctly? L2
Q4. "When Jenna met Amara, she gave her the report." Why is this sentence ambiguous, and how would you fix it? L4
🤔 One question that solves all four: Is the noun the pronoun refers to singular or plural in the SHSAT's eyes — and can you point to exactly one word it replaces?
