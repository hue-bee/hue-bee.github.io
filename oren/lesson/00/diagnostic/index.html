Zone 1 · Signed Numbers

Two Arrows on a Number Line

Memory shortcut
  • Same signs added/multiplied → keep the sign, combine size.
  • Different signs added → subtract smaller from larger, keep the "bigger" sign.
  • Two negatives multiplied → they cancel → positive.
  • Subtracting a negative = adding its opposite: 5 − (−2) = 5 + 2

Zone 3 · Percents — Top Target

"Of" Means Multiply

percent × whole = part
30% means 30 out of every 100
⚠️ SHSAT Trap: Discount ≠ Final Price
Test-makers love a wrong answer that's just the discount amount, not the new price. Always ask: "did the question ask what came OFF or what's LEFT?"

ELA · Strategy Debrief

Main Idea vs. Detail — Contrast on Purpose

🎯 Main Idea Checklist
  1. Main Idea = the whole passage's point, not one moment.
  2. Detail = one true fact stated directly in the text.
  3. Wrong Main Idea answers often quote ONE small detail and stretch it too far.
  4. Ask: "Does this answer cover the WHOLE passage, or just a piece?"
⚠️ Main Idea Trap: "True but Too Narrow"
Answer A is technically not proven false, but it's too narrow — it ignores the coach's role entirely. A trap answer is often true-sounding but incomplete.
